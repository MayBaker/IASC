Engine Options

The E92 BMW chassis is powered by some of the most legendary engines ever produced. Explore the twin-turbocharged N54 inline-6, a powerhouse of tuning potential, or dive into the naturally aspirated S65 V8 found in the iconic M3 model. Both engines represent the pinnacle of BMW's engineering excellence, offering thrilling performance and unique character.

N54 Engine

The N54 engine provides turbocharged power and immense tuning capabilities, making it a favorite among enthusiasts. Meanwhile, the S65 V8 delivers unmatched high-revving excitement, setting a benchmark for naturally aspirated performance.
