M3 Performance

The E92 M3 is a car that redefined performance in its class. Powered by the naturally aspirated S65 V8 engine, it delivers exhilarating power and a spine-tingling exhaust note. With precise handling, unmatched agility, and exceptional driving dynamics, the M3 is the ultimate expression of BMW's "Ultimate Driving Machine" philosophy.

Performance Highlights

  • S65 V8 Engine: A high-revving 4.0L V8 producing 414 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque.
  • 0-60 mph in 4.1 seconds: Exceptional acceleration for a naturally aspirated engine.
  • Balanced Chassis: Lightweight materials and near-perfect 50:50 weight distribution ensure precise handling.

Track-Ready Features

The E92 M3 comes equipped with advanced performance features like an Active M Differential, M Dynamic Mode, and a dual-clutch transmission (DCT) option, making it a beast both on the road and the racetrack.
